<p><p>Precision fiber optic couplers are devices used in optical networks to split or combine optical signals with high precision. There are various types of fiber optic couplers in the market, including Y couplers, T couplers, star couplers, tree couplers, and other specialized couplers. Y couplers split an input signal into two output signals, T couplers split a signal into three outputs, star couplers distribute signals to multiple outputs, and tree couplers combine signals from multiple inputs. Other specialized couplers include wavelength division multiplexers and polarization-maintaining couplers.</p></p>
