<p><ul><li>NiCd Battery</li><li>NiMH Battery</li><li>Li-Ion Battery</li><li>Lead-Acid Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emergency lighting systems typically use four main types of batteries: NiCd, NiMH, Li-Ion, and Lead-Acid. NiCd batteries are known for their long life, durability, and reliability. NiMH batteries offer higher energy density and longer life compared to NiCd. Li-Ion batteries are lightweight, have high energy density, and longer life. Lead-Acid batteries are cost-effective, reliable, and have a long lifespan. Each type of battery offers specific advantages and characteristics that suit different emergency lighting needs.</p></p>
