<p><p>Peltier Modules are electronic devices used for heating and cooling applications. There are two main types of Peltier Modules in the market - Single Stage Modules and Multistage Modules. Single Stage Modules are designed for simple cooling or heating applications, while Multistage Modules are used for more precise temperature control and higher efficiency. Both types of modules work by transferring heat from one side to the other when an electrical current is applied, allowing for customizable and efficient temperature management in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
