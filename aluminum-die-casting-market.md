<p><p>Aluminum die casting market types include low pressure die casting, high pressure die casting, and other markets. Low pressure die casting involves filling the mold from a pressurized crucible below the parting line, while high pressure die casting uses a high pressure injection of molten aluminum into a mold cavity. Other markets may include processes such as vacuum die casting, squeeze casting, and semi-solid die casting. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in the aluminum die casting industry.</p></p>
