<p><p>Dry topping dispensers are available in two main types: wall-mount and countertop. Wall-mount dispensers are fixed to the wall, saving valuable countertop space and allowing for easy access to toppings. These are commonly used in commercial kitchens and food service establishments. Countertop dispensers, on the other hand, sit directly on the counter and are more convenient for self-serve stations or smaller kitchen spaces. Both types offer efficient and organized ways to dispense dry toppings such as spices, seasonings, and nuts.</p></p>
