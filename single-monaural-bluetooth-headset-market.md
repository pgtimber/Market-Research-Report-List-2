<p><p>The Single Monaural Bluetooth Headset market consists of three main types: HSP (Headset Profile), HFP (Hands-Free Profile), and A2DP (Advanced Audio Distribution Profile). HSP is designed for voice communication, allowing users to make calls and use voice commands. HFP is similar to HSP but also includes additional features like call waiting and call forwarding. A2DP is focused on high-quality audio streaming, allowing users to listen to music or other audio content wirelessly. Each market type caters to specific needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
