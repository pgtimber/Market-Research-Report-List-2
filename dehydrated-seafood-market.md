Vacuum Dried seafood involves drying the seafood in a vacuum chamber to preserve its texture and nutrients. Sun Dried seafood is dried under sunlight. Hot Air Dried seafood is dried using hot air circulation. Others include various other drying methods used in the market.</p></p>
