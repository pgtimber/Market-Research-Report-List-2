<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Delay Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic wave delay lines can be categorized into two main types: surface acoustic wave (SAW) delay line and bulk acoustic wave (BAW) delay line. SAW delay lines utilize waves that travel along the surface of a piezoelectric material, whereas BAW delay lines use waves that travel through the bulk of the material. SAW delay lines are commonly used in filters and resonators, while BAW delay lines are often used in high-frequency applications such as RF filters and oscillators. Both types offer unique advantages and are used in various electronic devices.</p></p>
