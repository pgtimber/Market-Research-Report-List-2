<p><strong>The Fluorometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filter Fluorometer</li><li>Spectrofluorometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filter fluorometers are a type of fluorometer that operate by using a series of filters to select specific wavelengths of light for excitation and emission. This allows for precise measurements of fluorescence intensity at specific wavelengths. On the other hand, spectrofluorometers are a more advanced type of fluorometer that are capable of measuring fluorescence across a wide range of wavelengths simultaneously, providing detailed spectral information about the sample. Both types of fluorometers are widely used in various research and industrial applications for analyzing fluorescent materials.</p></p>
