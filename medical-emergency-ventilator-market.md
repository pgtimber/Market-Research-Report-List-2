<p><p>Medical Emergency Ventilator market includes various types of ventilators such as Continuous Positive Pressure Ventilator, Automatic Positive Pressure Breathing Machine, Bi-Level Positive Pressure Ventilator, and others. Continuous Positive Pressure Ventilators provide a constant flow of air to keep the airway open. Automatic Positive Pressure Breathing Machines deliver air at a preset pressure. Bi-Level Positive Pressure Ventilators deliver different pressures for inhalation and exhalation. Other types may include volume-cycled or pressure-cycled ventilators, depending on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
