<p><ul><li>Skimmed Evaporated Milk</li><li>Whole Evaporated Milk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Evaporated milk is available in two main market types: skimmed evaporated milk and whole evaporated milk. Skimmed evaporated milk is made by removing most of the fat content from regular milk, resulting in a lower fat product. Whole evaporated milk, on the other hand, retains all of the fat content from regular milk, giving it a richer and creamier texture. Both types of evaporated milk are commonly used in cooking and baking, as well as for adding creaminess to beverages like coffee.</p></p>
