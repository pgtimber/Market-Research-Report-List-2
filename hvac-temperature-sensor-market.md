<p><p>HVAC temperature sensors are crucial components of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. There are different types of temperature sensors used in HVAC systems including NTC sensors, PTC sensors, RTD sensors, thermocouples (TCs) sensors, and others. NTC sensors have a negative temperature coefficient, PTC sensors have a positive temperature coefficient, RTD sensors use a resistor to measure temperature, thermocouples operate based on the Seebeck effect, and other sensors utilize various technologies to accurately measure temperature in HVAC systems.</p></p>
