Medium voltage contactors are suitable for voltages between 1000V and 24V, commonly found in industrial and commercial settings. Low voltage contactors are designed for voltages below 1000V, typically used in residential applications. Each type of contactor is tailored to meet the specific voltage requirements of different electrical systems, ensuring efficient and reliable operation.</p></p>
