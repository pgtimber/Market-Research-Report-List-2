<p><p>Helium flow meters are used for measuring the flow rate of helium gas in various industries. There are three main types of helium flow meters in the market: hydraulic, thermal, and others. Hydraulic flow meters measure flow rate by forcing the gas through a small orifice and measuring the pressure drop. Thermal flow meters measure flow rate by using a heated sensor to measure the heat loss caused by the gas flow. Other types of flow meters may use different principles or technologies to measure helium flow rate.</p></p>
