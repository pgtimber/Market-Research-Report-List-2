<p><p>Biological control market types include predatory mites, insects, nematodes, and other organisms that are used to control pests in agriculture. Predatory mites are commonly used to control spider mites, thrips, and other small pests. Insects such as ladybugs and lacewings are used to control aphids and other plant-eating insects. Nematodes are microscopic worms that parasitize pest insects in the soil. Other biological control agents include fungi, bacteria, and viruses that can be used to control a wide range of pests in an environmentally friendly manner.</p></p>
