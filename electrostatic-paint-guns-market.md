<p><p>Electrostatic paint guns are tools used for applying a coat of paint to a surface using an electric charge. Two common types in the market include pneumatic electrostatic paint guns and airless electrostatic paint guns. Pneumatic guns use compressed air to atomize the paint and apply a fine mist, while airless guns use high pressure to force paint through a small opening, resulting in a solid stream. Both types offer efficient and uniform coverage, making them popular choices in the painting industry.</p></p>
