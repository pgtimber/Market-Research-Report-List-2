<p><p>Temperature and humidity sensors come in various types in the market to cater to different applications and requirements. The capacitive type sensors measure temperature and humidity using a capacitive sensor element, which provides high accuracy and reliability. Resistive type sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance to measure temperature and humidity, offering cost-effective solutions. Other types of sensors may include optical sensors or thermal conductivity sensors, each with its own advantages and limitations. Customers can choose the best sensor type based on their specific needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
