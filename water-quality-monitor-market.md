<p><p>The Water Quality Monitor market consists of two main types: Single Parameter Monitors and Multi-Parameter Monitors. Single Parameter Monitors are designed to measure specific water quality parameters such as pH, dissolved oxygen, or turbidity. They are typically more affordable and simpler to use. On the other hand, Multi-Parameter Monitors are capable of measuring multiple parameters simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of water quality. They are often used in more complex monitoring applications where a broader range of data is needed for analysis and decision-making.</p></p>
