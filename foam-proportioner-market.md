<p><strong>The Foam Proportioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Foam Proportioner</li><li>Ring Pump Foam Proportioner</li><li>In-Line Foam Proportioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foam Proportioners are used in firefighting to mix water with foam concentrate and create fire-resistant foam. Pressure Foam Proportioners use the pressure from the water supply to mix the foam concentrate. Ring Pump Foam Proportioners utilize a pump to mix the water and foam concentrate. In-Line Foam Proportioners work by injecting the foam concentrate into the water stream. Each type of Foam Proportioner has its own advantages and is used in different scenarios based on the specific requirements of the firefighting operation.</p></p>
