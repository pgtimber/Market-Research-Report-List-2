<p><p>Fire alarm detection systems come in various types, including photoelectric, ionization, and dual sensor detectors. Photoelectric detectors use a light beam to detect smoke particles, while ionization detectors use radioactive particles to detect smoke. Dual sensor detectors combine both technologies for increased accuracy in detecting different types of fires. Each type has its own benefits and drawbacks, and the choice of system depends on factors such as the specific environment and the level of protection required.</p></p>
