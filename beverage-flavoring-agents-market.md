<p><p>Beverage flavoring agents can be categorized into three main types: natural, artificial, and nature-identical. Natural flavoring agents are derived from natural sources such as fruits, vegetables, and herbs. Artificial flavoring agents are chemically synthesized to mimic natural flavors. Nature-identical flavoring agents are chemically identical to natural flavors, but they are not extracted from the original sources. Each type of flavoring agent offers unique benefits and is used in the beverage industry to enhance the taste and aroma of products.</p></p>
