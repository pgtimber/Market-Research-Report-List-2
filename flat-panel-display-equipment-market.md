<p><strong>The Flat Panel Display Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation</li><li>Photolithography (Exposure)</li><li>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD)</li><li>Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA)</li><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)</li><li>Wet Etch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat Panel Display Equipment Market Types include various technologies used in the manufacturing process of flat panel displays. Evaporation is used for depositing thin films onto substrates. Photolithography involves exposing photoresist-coated substrates to light to create patterns. Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) is a method of depositing thin films by condensing vaporized material onto a substrate. Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA) is used to enhance the performance of flat panel displays. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) is a process of depositing thin films using chemical reactions. Wet Etch involves selectively removing materials using liquid chemicals.</p></p>
