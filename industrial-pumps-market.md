<p><p>Industrial pumps can be classified into four main types: centrifugal, reciprocating, rotary, and diaphragm pumps. Centrifugal pumps use rotating impellers to create flow and increase pressure. Reciprocating pumps use pistons or plungers to push fluid through the system. Rotary pumps use rotating mechanisms to move fluid through a system. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to displace fluid. Each type of pump has distinct advantages and is utilized in various industries based on specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
