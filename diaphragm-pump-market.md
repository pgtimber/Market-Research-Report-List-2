<p><p>Diaphragm pump market consists of various types such as Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD), Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD), and Piston Diaphragm Pumps. AODD pumps use compressed air for operation, making them suitable for remote locations or hazardous environments. EODD pumps are powered by electricity, offering consistent and efficient performance. Piston diaphragm pumps use a reciprocating piston to move fluid, ideal for high-pressure applications. Each type caters to different needs and industries within the diaphragm pump market.</p></p>
