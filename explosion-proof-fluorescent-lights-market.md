<p><ul><li>Conventional Fluorescent Lights</li><li>Non-polar Fluorescent Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Explosion proof fluorescent lights are categorized into two main types, conventional fluorescent lights and non-polar fluorescent lights. Conventional fluorescent lights are designed with traditional wiring methods and must be installed in specific configurations to prevent explosions. On the other hand, non-polar fluorescent lights are designed with non-polarized wiring, allowing them to be installed in any orientation without the risk of explosion. Both types of lights are used in hazardous environments where the presence of flammable gases or vapors poses a threat.</p></p>
