<p><p>Degassing valves are essential components used in packaging to release excess gas from the package and maintain freshness of the product. There are two main types of degassing valves in the market: Filter integrated valves and Non-filter integrated valves. Filter integrated valves have a filter to prevent entry of contaminants, while Non-filter integrated valves do not have a filter but still release gas from the package. Both types play a crucial role in extending the shelf life of products by allowing for the release of gases produced during packaging.</p></p>
