<p><p>Dimmer light switches come in various types such as rheostat, coil-rotation transformer, solid-state dimmers, and others. Rheostat dimmer switches use variable resistors to control the brightness of the light. Coil-rotation transformer dimmers regulate the voltage using a coil and rotating switch. Solid-state dimmers use electronic components to adjust the light intensity more efficiently. Other types of dimmers include digital dimmers and integrated dimming systems. Each type offers a unique method of controlling and adjusting the lighting in a space.</p></p>
