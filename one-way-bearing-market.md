<p><p>One Way Bearings come in various market types, including Wedge Design, which utilizes inclined planes to allow for rotation in one direction only. Self-locking Angle Design features a mechanism that prevents reverse rotation at a specific angle. Slope and Roller Design incorporates a combination of slopes and rollers to achieve unidirectional rotation. Each of these designs offers unique advantages and caters to different applications, making them versatile options in the One Way Bearing market.</p></p>
