<p><p>Online Transformer Monitoring System market is categorized into multiple types based on the monitoring techniques used. Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA) monitors gases dissolved in transformer oil to detect faults. Partial Discharge (PD) monitoring focuses on detecting localized insulation breakdown. Bushing Monitoring (BM) tracks the condition of bushings. Thermal Fault Characterization (TFC) detects abnormal temperature patterns in transformers. Other monitoring techniques may include moisture content monitoring, oil quality monitoring, and acoustic monitoring. Each type of monitoring provides valuable insights to ensure optimal transformer performance and prevent potential failures.</p></p>
