<p><p>Food texture market types include thickener, gelling agents, emulsifiers, stabilizers, and other additives. Thickener additives are used to increase the viscosity of food products. Gelling agents help in forming gels or solidify the product. Emulsifiers are used to mix ingredients that do not naturally combine. Stabilizers help maintain the texture and consistency of food products. Other additives may include anti-caking agents, bulking agents, and humectants, each serving a specific purpose in enhancing food texture.</p></p>
