<p><p>Thermoforming packing machines are available in three main types - Automatic, Semi-automatic, and Manual. Automatic machines require minimal human intervention and operate independently once set up, making them efficient for high-volume production. Semi-automatic machines require some human involvement, such as loading materials or monitoring the process, offering a balance between automation and control. Manual machines are operated entirely by hand and are suitable for small-scale production or prototyping. Each type caters to different production needs and levels of automation.</p></p>
