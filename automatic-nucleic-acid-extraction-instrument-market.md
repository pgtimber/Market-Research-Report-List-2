<p><p>Automatic Nucleic Acid Extraction Instruments are used for efficient isolation of DNA and RNA from biological samples. Two common types in the market are the Spin Column Method and Magnetic Bead Method. The Spin Column Method involves centrifugation of the sample through a column to separate nucleic acids, while the Magnetic Bead Method uses magnetic beads to capture nucleic acids for extraction. Both methods are automated, providing fast and reliable results for various molecular biology applications.</p></p>
