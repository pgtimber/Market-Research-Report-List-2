<p><p>Gas carburizing furnaces are widely used in the heat treatment industry. The Roller Hearth Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace is a type of furnace that uses a roller conveyor system to move parts through the heating and carburizing process continuously. On the other hand, the Push Plate Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace is a type of furnace where parts are pushed through the heating and carburizing process on a plate conveyor system. Both types of furnaces are efficient and effective in providing uniform carburizing results for various industrial applications.</p></p>
