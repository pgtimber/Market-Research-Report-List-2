<p><p>Non-lead package leadframe market is divided into two major types: Stamping Process Lead Frame and Etching Process Lead Frame. Stamping process lead frame involves forming lead frames through stamping metal sheets, while etching process lead frame involves chemically etching the lead frames from metal sheets. Both methods have their own advantages and applications in the semiconductor industry. Stamping process lead frames are known for their high precision and efficiency, while etching process lead frames are preferred for their ability to produce intricate designs and patterns.</p></p>
