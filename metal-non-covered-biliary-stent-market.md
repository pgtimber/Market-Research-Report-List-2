<p><strong>The Metal Non Covered Biliary Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal non-covered biliary stents are used to relieve obstructions in the bile duct. They are categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types based on their material composition. Absorbable stents gradually dissolve in the body over time, providing temporary support until the obstruction resolves. Non-absorbable stents, on the other hand, remain in place permanently to maintain bile flow. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the severity of the obstruction.</p></p>
