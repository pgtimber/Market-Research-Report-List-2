<p><p>Swivel chair gas springs can be categorized into two main types - Elastic Lock and Rigid Lock. Elastic Lock gas springs allow for easy adjustment of the chair height by simply applying pressure, which activates the gas spring mechanism. On the other hand, Rigid Lock gas springs require the user to manually unlock and lock the height adjustment, providing a more stable and secure position once locked in place. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various swivel chair applications based on the desired level of flexibility and stability.</p></p>
