<p><strong>The Cable Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC</li><li>PTC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cable sensors are divided into NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient), PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient), and Other types. NTC cable sensors have a resistance that decreases with an increase in temperature, making them suitable for temperature sensing applications. In contrast, PTC cable sensors have a resistance that increases with rising temperature, making them ideal for self-regulating heating systems. Other types of cable sensors include RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) and thermocouples, which have unique characteristics and applications in temperature measurement and control.</p></p>
