<p><p>The Handheld Core Drill market includes two main types: Dry Core Drill and Wet Core Drill. Dry Core Drills are used for drilling into materials such as concrete without the use of water for cooling and lubrication. Wet Core Drills, on the other hand, require water to cool the drill bit and flush out debris during drilling. Both types of core drills are widely used in construction, mining, and other industries for creating precise holes in hard materials.</p></p>
