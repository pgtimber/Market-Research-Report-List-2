<p><p>FPC Antennas market can be categorized into two main types: Internal Antennas and External Antennas. Internal Antennas are designed to be integrated within the electronic device, providing a sleek and compact design. They are commonly used in smartphones, tablets, and other portable devices. On the other hand, External Antennas are separate components attached to the outside of the device, offering better reception and signal strength. They are often used in routers, modems, and other stationary devices where performance is a priority over aesthetics.</p></p>
