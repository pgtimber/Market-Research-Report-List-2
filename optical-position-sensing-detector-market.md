<p><strong>The Optical Position Sensing Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Cell</li><li>Quadrant</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Position Sensing Detectors are commonly categorized into three main types: Dual Cell, Quadrant, and Others. </p><p>Dual Cell detectors utilize two cells to provide directional information based on the difference in signals received by each cell. Quadrant detectors have four separate cells to accurately determine the position of the light source. </p><p>The "Others" category may include various types of position sensing detectors that do not fit into the Dual Cell or Quadrant classifications, such as linear array detectors or segmented detectors.</p></p>
