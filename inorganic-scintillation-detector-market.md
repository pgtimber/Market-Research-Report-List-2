<p><strong>The Inorganic Scintillation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Scintillation Detector</li><li>Liquid Scintillation Detector</li><li>Gaseous Scintillation Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inorganic scintillation detectors can be classified into three main types: solid scintillation detectors, liquid scintillation detectors, and gaseous scintillation detectors. Solid scintillation detectors contain a crystalline material that emits light when struck by ionizing radiation. Liquid scintillation detectors use liquid scintillators to detect radiation by measuring the light emitted. Gaseous scintillation detectors utilize gases that emit light when ionized by radiation. Each type of detector has its own advantages and limitations, providing options for a wide range of radiation detection applications.</p></p>
