<p><p>Beamsplitters are optical devices used to split a beam of light into two or more separate beams. There are three main types of beamsplitters in the market: Plate Type, Pellicle Type, and Cube Type. Plate Type beamsplitters are thin plates with a partially reflective coating, allowing them to split light based on wavelength or polarization. Pellicle Type beamsplitters are thin membranes that split light without causing interference. Cube Type beamsplitters are solid cubes with a partially reflective coating on one face, splitting light into two orthogonal beams.</p></p>
