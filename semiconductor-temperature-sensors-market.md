<p><strong>The Semiconductor Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Current Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Digital Output Temperature Sensors</li><li>Resistance Output Silicon Temperature Sensors</li><li>Diode Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor temperature sensors come in various types based on their output signals. Voltage output temperature sensors produce a voltage proportional to the temperature, while current output sensors yield a current signal. Digital output sensors provide temperature readings digitally. Resistance output silicon temperature sensors change resistance with temperature variations. Diode temperature sensors utilize the temperature-dependent voltage across a diode. Each type caters to different applications and industries, providing accurate temperature measurement and control solutions.</p></p>
