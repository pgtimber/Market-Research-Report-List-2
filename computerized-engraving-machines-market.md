Laser engraving machines use a high-powered laser beam to engrave materials such as metal, wood, and plastic with precision and accuracy. Mechanical engraving machines use a physical tool, such as a rotating cutter or diamond tip, to engrave materials. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and jewelry for marking products, creating personalized items, and producing intricate designs.</p></p>
