<p><p>Linear guide market includes various types such as ball guide rail, roller guide rail, needle guide rail, and others. Ball guide rail utilizes rolling balls to reduce friction and enable smooth motion, roller guide rail uses cylindrical rollers for linear motion, and needle guide rail employs needle rollers for precise movement. Other types may include air bearings, magnetic bearings, or hydrostatic systems. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different applications based on specific requirements for precision, load capacity, and speed.</p></p>
