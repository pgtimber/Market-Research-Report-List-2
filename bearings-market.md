<p><p>Bearings are used in various industries to support rotational or linear movements. The ball bearing market consists of bearings that use spherical balls to reduce friction between two moving parts. They are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications due to their high efficiency. On the other hand, the roller bearing market includes bearings that use cylindrical or tapered rollers instead of balls. Roller bearings are preferred for heavy-duty applications as they can support higher radial and axial loads compared to ball bearings.</p></p>
