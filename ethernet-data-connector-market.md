<p><strong>The Ethernet Data Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug-In</li><li>Push-Pull</li><li>Bayonet</li><li>Crimp</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ethernet data connectors come in various types to suit different applications. Plug-in connectors are easy to install and remove, making them convenient for frequent connections. Push-pull connectors offer a secure connection with a simple push-and-rotate mechanism. Bayonet connectors provide a quick and reliable twist-lock connection. Crimp connectors require manual tools for installation, ensuring a solid connection. Other market types may include soldered connectors or screw-on connectors, offering different levels of security and ease of use for Ethernet data connections.</p></p>
