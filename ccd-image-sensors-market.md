<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CCD Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Full Frame Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li><li>Frame Interline Transfer CCD Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frame Transfer CCD Sensor is a type of image sensor that transfers the charges from the pixels to a storage area before readout, allowing for continuous image capture. Full Frame Transfer CCD Sensor has a dedicated storage area for each pixel, enabling higher resolution images. Interline Transfer CCD Sensor transfers charges directly to the readout register, providing fast image capture. Frame Interline Transfer CCD Sensor combines the features of frame transfer and interline transfer sensors, allowing for high-speed imaging with minimal lag.</p></p>
