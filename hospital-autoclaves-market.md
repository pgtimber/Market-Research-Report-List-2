<p><p>Hospital autoclaves market can be categorized into three main types: Manual Hospital Autoclaves, Fully Automatic Hospital Autoclaves, and Semi-Automatic Hospital Autoclaves. Manual autoclaves require constant supervision and manual control of sterilization process, while fully automatic autoclaves operate autonomously with pre-programmed cycles. Semi-automatic autoclaves combine elements of both manual and automatic controls, requiring some user input but also offering automated features. Each type of autoclave caters to different needs and preferences of healthcare facilities, depending on their sterilization requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
