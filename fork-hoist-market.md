<p><p>Fork hoists are commonly available in two main types: Counterbalanced Fork Hoists and Warehouse Fork Hoists. Counterbalanced Fork Hoists are designed with a weight at the back of the truck to offset the load weight at the front, allowing for easy maneuverability in tight spaces. Warehouse Fork Hoists are designed for use in narrow aisles and high shelves in warehouses, with features such as electric motors and improved stability to maximize efficiency and productivity in a warehouse setting.</p></p>
