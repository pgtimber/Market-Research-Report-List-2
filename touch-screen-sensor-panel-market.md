<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Resistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen sensor panel market is divided into two main types: capacitive and resistive. Capacitive touch screens are more responsive and provide better clarity, using the electrical properties of the human body to detect touch. Resistive touch screens, on the other hand, rely on pressure to register touch, making them more durable and able to be used with gloves or styluses. Both technologies have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
