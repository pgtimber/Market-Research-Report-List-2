<p><p>Automotive NTC thermistors come in various types based on their construction and design. Radial type thermistors have a cylindrical shape with leads extending from each end. Diode type thermistors are specifically designed to mimic the electrical characteristics of a diode, providing accurate temperature readings. SMD type thermistors are surface-mount devices that are soldered onto circuit boards. There are also other types of thermistors available in the market, each with unique features and applications tailored to different automotive needs.</p></p>
