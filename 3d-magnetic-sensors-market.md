<p><ul><li>Linear 3D Magnetic Sensors</li><li>Rotary 3D Magnetic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear 3D magnetic sensors are used to measure linear movement and displacement in all three dimensions. They are commonly used in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronic applications. On the other hand, rotary 3D magnetic sensors are designed to measure rotational movement along three axes. These sensors are frequently used in robotics, aerospace, and medical industries for applications such as joystick control, steering wheel position sensing, and motion tracking. Both types of sensors offer high accuracy, reliability, and durability for precise measurement in various applications.</p></p>
