<p><p>NEV IGBT Modules Heatsink market offers two main types of heatsinks: Air-Cooled Heatsinks and Water-Cooled Heatsinks. Air-Cooled Heatsinks use natural convection or forced airflow to dissipate heat, making them suitable for moderate heat dissipation requirements. On the other hand, Water-Cooled Heatsinks use water circulation to effectively remove heat, making them ideal for applications with high heat dissipation demands. Both types of heatsinks play a crucial role in maintaining the optimal operating temperature of NEV IGBT modules, ensuring their reliable performance.</p></p>
